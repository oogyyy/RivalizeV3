# Dust2 — CT-Side Default

**Map overview (CT-perspective):** CTs win Dust2 by contesting Long early with an AWP pick, denying B Tunnels with utility, and keeping mid control to prevent a Short rush. The AWP at Long is the single highest-value position on the map; losing that duel opens A completely.

---

## Starting Positions

| Role | Callout | Anchor Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| AWPer | Long (CT / Pit area) | Opens Long duel; can reposition to Short or Catwalk after pick |
| Entry | Short (Catwalk) | Watches Catwalk; denies T mid access; calls tunnels info |
| Support | A-Site (Goose / CT area) | Anchors A; crossfire with AWPer; throws flashes for AWP duel |
| Lurker | B-Site (Platform) | Anchors B; calls Tunnels approach timing; holds Upper B |
| IGL | Mid (Upper Tunnels wall / B doors) | Collects B+mid info; rotates to weakest side |

**Standard setup: AWP Long, 1 Short, 1 A-Site, 1 B-Site, 1 Mid (IGL)**

---

## Early Map Control (1:40 → 1:10)

AWPer peeks Long CT at 1:43 — the first Long peek is the most important action of the round. Support flashes Long for the AWPer's peek. Entry aggressively checks Catwalk at 1:40 to deny T mid control. IGL throws an HE into B Tunnels upper at 1:50 to punish a fast rush.

## Rotation Rules

| Trigger | Response |
|---|---|
| AWPer gets Long pick | AWPer repositions to Car; Support moves up to Long for crossfire hold |
| Entry calls "2+ T mid catwalk" | IGL rotates from Mid to Short; Entry falls back to CT; AWPer stays Long |
| Lurker calls "smoke + flash B tunnels" | IGL pushes into B from Mid; Lurker holds platform; do not rotate A yet |
| Support calls "smoke Catwalk + smoke CT" simultaneously | Full A execute coming — AWPer falls back to CT Spawn; IGL rotates A; Lurker stays B |
| IGL calls "5 B" (confirms all T utility B side) | AWPer rotates through mid to Short; Entry stays CT for late A defense |

## Crossfire Setups

**Setup 1 — Long CT + Car:**
- **AWPer** holds from **Long CT** (main angle)
- **Support** holds from **Car** or **Pit**
- T entry must peek two separate angles; Car player forces the trade even if AWPer is killed

**Setup 2 — B-Site Platform + B-Doors:**
- **Lurker** holds from **B Platform** (covering tunnels entry)
- **IGL** holds **B Doors** (or upper B)
- Entry from Tunnels must clear platform first before IGL; creates forced 2-angle problem

**Setup 3 — Goose + Long (A retake):**
- **Support** falls back to **Goose** area
- **AWPer** pushes up **Long** after rotation
- T can't clear both simultaneously during retake; Goose close-hold is lethal

---

## Retake — A Site

**Scenario:** T executes with CT smoke, Car smoke, and Catwalk smoke.

1. **AWPer** pushes from **Long** (smokes will have cleared by retake time) — watch for T at Goose
2. **Support** re-enters from **CT Spawn**, pops flash into site — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+dust2+flash+ct+spawn+a+retake)
3. **Entry** enters from **Short into Catwalk** → clears Pit + near side
4. **IGL** follows Support, defuses from CT side of bomb
5. **Lurker** stays B to prevent a T lurk; enters only if fully safe

---

## Retake — B Site

**Scenario:** T executes B Tunnels with CT window smoke and back-site smoke.

1. **IGL** pushes from **Mid** through **Short → B Doors** — fastest B retake route — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+dust2+b+retake+short+b+doors)
2. **Lurker** already on B — pops flash from **Platform** into site, then pushes in
3. **AWPer** rotates through mid (Catwalk) into Short; provides crossfire from B Doors
4. IGL + Lurker retake together; AWPer holds the T exit (B Tunnels) to prevent re-entry
5. **Support** (from A) rotates only if first 3 players confirm entry is clean

## Economy

- **CT pistol:** Lurker buys armor + P250 (B is the most likely pistol rush target). IGL buys HE for B Tunnels. AWPer buys P250. Others buy armor.
- **Anti-eco:** AWPer full buys; others buy SMGs. AWP holds Long passively; don't over-extend.
- **After 2 consecutive losses:** Coordinate economy — either all rifles or all pistols. IGL makes the call before the buy phase.
