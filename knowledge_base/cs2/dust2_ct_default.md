# CT-Side Default for de_dust2

#### Introduction
Our CT-side default on Dust2 relies on an early AWP presence on Long, strong B site anchoring, and using Mid as the rotation backbone. Information from Long and B dictates where the IGL commits the team during the mid-round phase.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [AWPer]: Flash own corner at CT Long to safely push and take Long control early ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [B Anchor]: Molly top of B Tunnels (Upper entrance) from B Site to slow a fast B rush ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Short Player]: Flash B Doors from Window position to catch early T Mid movement ▶ Watch lineup.

#### Default Positions
▸ [AWPer]: Play CT Long (aggressive) or CT Cross (passive) — punish T Long entry and call positions.
▸ [Rifler 1]: Hold Short from A Short or B Doors / Window to contest Mid control.
▸ [Support 1]: Anchor A Site from CT angle or Pit, ready to crossfire with AWPer on Long.
▸ [Support 2]: Anchor B Site from Goose or Car position — deep B hold to deny bomb plant.
▸ [IGL]: Play B Site / Mid region — read T approach direction and call rotations.

#### Rotation Rules
▸ If T AWP is confirmed at Long: Support 1 assists AWPer from A Short. IGL holds B solo briefly.
▸ If heavy B Tunnel movement is heard: IGL + Support 2 hold B. AWPer stays Long to delay an A open.
▸ If Catwalk + Long are smoked simultaneously (A execute): all CT-side players rotate A. B is abandoned temporarily.
▸ If B is planted but only 1-2 Ts alive: AWPer rotates B via CT Spawn; Short rifler drops to B Doors.

#### Crossfire Setups
▸ Long + A Short: AWPer holds Long angle from CT Cross; rifler holds A Short entry — T committed to Long is caught from two directions simultaneously.
▸ B Site + Goose: One B anchor plays Car / B Site (mid-range); second plays behind Goose (close range). T entering B must clear both positions.
▸ Window + B Doors: Rifler at Window watches both B Doors and Mid, creating a dangerous crossfire against any Mid / Short push.

#### Retake Coordination
▸ A Site Retake: AWPer from Long / CT Cross; rifler drops from Short. Pop flash into A Site from Short. Clear Car first, then the default plant spot.
▸ B Site Retake: IGL pushes from CT Spawn through B Doors. AWPer rotates from Long via CT. Flash from B Doors into B Site. Clear Goose angle first, then back of site.

#### Adapting to T Reads
▸ If Ts are fast rushing B Tunnels:
- B anchor retreats to deep B hold (behind boxes). IGL rotates immediately.
- Do not fight in Tunnels — let them come out and take the crossfire at B Site.
▸ If Ts are slow defaulting with Long + Mid setup:
- AWPer must contest Long early. Short player peeks to gather Catwalk / Mid information.
- Stack A if information suggests heavy Long presence — let B anchor hold solo while the rest collapse.
