# Dust2 — T-Side Default

**Map overview (T-perspective):** Dust2 is a linear 3-lane map. Long A control is the highest-value opening move — it exposes CT, shortens execute distance, and creates a split threat. B is the fastest execute but the most telegraphed. Mid catwalk gives T site access to both B (via Short) and A (via CT catwalk). The best T teams control Long or Mid before committing.

---

## Starting Positions

| Role | Callout | Opening Job |
|---|---|---|
| AWPer | T-Spawn → Long Doors | Peek Long Doors or hold for Long push |
| Entry | T-Spawn → Long (with AWPer) | Second man on Long; takes fights after AWPer opens |
| Support | T-Spawn → Mid Doors | Smoke Catwalk to mid; unlock T Mid for Lurker |
| Lurker | T-Spawn → Mid Doors | Take mid catwalk after Support smokes; control Short |
| IGL | T-Spawn → Long OR B | Calls based on Long vs B pressure; gathers mid info |

**Default split: 3 Long, 1 Mid (Lurker), 1 B (IGL for information)**

---

## Default Phase (1:40 → 1:00)

AWPer peeks Long Doors at 1:42 — a pick here opens Long A completely. If no pick, Entry advances to **Pit** and AWPer falls back to **Car**. Support smokes Catwalk at 1:40 to give Lurker safe Mid entry. IGL walks B Upper to count CTs and reports back.

### Entry & Trade Plan

3. **AWPer** pops flash and follows, holds Long distance/catwalk angle
4. **Support** trails, plants at default (behind Goose or CT side)

- **Support** → Pit (planter; repositions to cover retake)
- **IGL** → CT catwalk entry (intercepts rotating CT)
- **Lurker** → Short (full map coverage; cuts B→A rotation)

- **Lurker** → Short (cuts B doors CT rotation)
- **Support** → B Tunnels (time-waste, watches back)

## Economy Considerations

- **Pistol T (Round 1):** 3 rush B Tunnels (Entry, IGL, Lurker); AWPer + Support take Long for a delayed A pressure. Do NOT split 5 ways on pistol.
- **Anti-Eco:** Fast rush B with 4 players; AWPer holds Long in case 1 CT tries to contest.
- **Force Buy:** AK rush Long — no utility needed; force a quick Long pick before CTs are set up.
