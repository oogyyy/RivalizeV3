# Overpass — T-Side Default

**Map overview (T-perspective):** Overpass is a high-utility map where B-Main is the dominant T route (tight but rewarding) and A-Short/Connector gives an aggressive AWP position. The optimal default is Connector + A pressure with a Lurker on B for information, then committing to a coordinated execute.

---

## Starting Positions

| Role | Callout | Opening Job |
|---|---|---|
| AWPer | T-Spawn → Connector | Opens Connector AWP duel; threatens A-Short |
| Entry | T-Spawn → B-Main | Leads B-Main push; applies pressure toward Fountain |
| Support | T-Spawn → B-Main (with Entry) | Throws B utility; enables Entry's push |
| Lurker | T-Spawn → Connector (with AWPer) | Follows AWPer; threatens A-Short or cut mid |
| IGL | T-Spawn → B-Main OR Connector | Calls B vs A based on entry's report and AWPer info |

**Default split: 2 Connector/A-Short, 3 B-Main**

---

## Default Phase (1:40 → 1:00)

AWPer opens Connector at 1:42. Entry + Support walk B-Main and apply utility pressure at Bank. IGL reads whether CT is stacking B or leaving A vulnerable.

**Utility in this phase:**
- **Support** smokes **Bank** from **B-Main** at 1:40 to deny CT Bank aggression — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+overpass+smoke+b+main+bank)
- **Support** throws molotov into **B-Main chokepoint** at 1:50 to flush CT early aggression — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+overpass+molotov+b+main+ct+peek)
- **AWPer** flashes **Connector** from **T-Spawn** at 1:42 for the peek — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+overpass+flash+connector+t+side)

### Entry & Trade Plan
1. Playground + Fountain + Bank smokes all up (0:54)
2. **Entry** pops flash and enters B through Monster at 0:52
3. **IGL** follows, clears Fountain area (CT side smoked)
4. **AWPer** enters last, holds long B angle
5. **Support** plants at Default (Fountain-side)
6. **Lurker** (from Connector) cuts A → B CT rotation via Short

### Post-Plant
- **Entry** → B close (behind cover near bomb)
- **IGL** → Fountain exit (holds CT retake)
- **AWPer** → B-Main (holds B doors retake entry)
- **Support** → B back corner (time-waste)
- **Lurker** → Short/A-Short (cuts full rotation)

- **AWPer** → Connector (crossfire)

## Economy Considerations

- **Pistol T:** 3 rush B-Main (Entry, IGL, Lurker); AWPer + Support hold Connector. Execute B on pistol with deagle/P250.
- **Force Buy:** Rush B-Main with AKs — short execute distance.
- **Anti-Eco:** Fast B rush — 4 players through B-Main.
