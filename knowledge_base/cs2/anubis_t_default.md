# T-Side Default for de_anubis

#### Introduction
Anubis is the most T-sided map in the active pool (55–57% T win rate at pro level). Our default exploits this through a B 3+2 Canal split — the map's signature execute — where 3 players hit B Main while 2 move through the Canal (Water) to arrive at Dark simultaneously. The January 2026 update flipped the mid door orientation and relocated the bridge drop hole, making Mid Connector smoke mandatory before any mid movement.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [Support 1]: Throw Mid Connector smoke from Alley (stand at Alley corner near wooden plank, aim at top of mid door frame, jump-throw) — mandatory post-January 2026 update; mid door flip gives CTs far more early vision without this smoke ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Support 2]: Throw B Connector smoke from B Main approach to block CT aggression through B Connector and allow safe B Main advancement early ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Entry]: Flash B Main entry from T Spawn to assist teammates entering the B Main corridor safely ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [IGL]: Molly B Site Flowers corner or CT-side anchor position to deny aggressive CT B anchor setup before B execute begins.

#### Info-Gathering Positions
▸ [Entry]: Lead B Main — advances into B Main corridor and calls CT commitment levels (single CT, stacked B, or passive).
▸ [Support 1]: Second body in B Main — provides entry flashes for Entry fragger; holds B Main depth if CT wins the opening duel; keeps B threat alive.
▸ [AWPer]: Take T Mid Alley → Bridge — contests mid after Connector smoke lands; takes aggressive Bridge peek at CT; post-January 2026, this requires Connector smoke before crossing.
▸ [IGL]: Hold A Main approach — reads CT Palace and A Main numbers; provides the A vs. B call based on whether A is passive or defended.
▸ [Lurker]: Take Water / Canal route — moves through Canal into Dark; creates simultaneous B split or holds as late-round A lurk threat; must walk (not run) through Water to avoid audible footstep callout.

#### Default Split Routes
▸ Default B 3+2 Canal Split (signature pro execute): 3 players hit B Main with Heaven (from Ruins — close range, no lineup needed) + Palace + B Connector smokes. 2 players move through Canal to Dark simultaneously, arriving at B Site from the CT angle at the same moment B Main entry begins. CTs cannot hold both angles with standard B defense.
▸ Default A 3+2 Connector Split: 3 players push A Main with Palace + CT A Heaven smokes. 2 players advance via Mid Connector (Connector smoke already in place) to arrive at A Site from Connector angle simultaneously. Lurker enters from A Short to split the site from a third angle.

#### Timing Breakpoints
▸ First 30s: Mid Connector smoke in immediately (January 2026 change — non-negotiable). B Main pressure applied by Entry + Support. AWPer takes Bridge / mid info. IGL reads A Main. Do NOT send Canal player through Water without IGL awareness — footsteps are audible to CTs.
▸ Mid-Round (30s-45s): If B Main numbers are low (Entry reports single CT or none) and Canal player has Water control — commit B 3+2 split with Heaven + Palace + B Connector smokes. If IGL reports passive A with CT over-committed to B — execute A 3+2 through Main + Connector.
▸ Late Round (45s+): AWPer holds Bridge for a late B Main entrance pick. If no pick is available, set up a late B execute using Ruins Heaven smoke (instant throw, no lineup needed) and remaining players. Do NOT send Canal player into Dark after 45s without B Main supporting — CT can hold Dark alone.

#### Adapting to CT Reads
▸ If CTs are aggressive in B Main (early CT peek or rush into B Main corridor):
- Double B Main smokes seal the corridor. Entry holds inside and lets CT come to the crossfire.
- Flash from the CT B smoke position to punish the peeking CT. Redirect 2 Canal players to A via Mid Connector while B CT is over-extended.
▸ If CTs are stacking B with 3+ players:
- Push 3 players through A Main with Palace + CT Heaven smokes. AWPer provides mid pressure via Bridge.
- Keep 2 players in B Main as a delayed threat — CT cannot fully abandon B to retake A without risking a B fast-plant on their rotation.
▸ If CTs are playing aggressive mid after January 2026 update (new door orientation gives earlier vision):
- Mid Connector smoke already in place — AWPer holds counter-peek from T Alley cover.
- Abandon mid and redirect full resources to B 3+2 split; do not fight mid without additional support smokes.

#### Key Smoke Lineups (Pro Reference)
▸ Mid Connector smoke: Alley corner near wooden plank — aim at top of mid door frame — jump-throw. Essential post-January 2026 door flip.
▸ B Heaven smoke (B execute): From Ruins / B Main approach — close-range throw, no specific lineup needed — blocks Heaven CT position completely.
▸ Palace smoke (B execute): Stand at second wall bulge in Ruins — crosshair on left side of design pattern ahead — normal throw. Blocks CT Palace rotation.
▸ B Connector smoke (B execute): T side / B Main — aimed at B Connector passage — prevents CT rotating through B Connector onto B site retake.
▸ CT A Heaven smoke (A execute): From A Main — aimed at A Heaven / Temple area — blocks CT AWP from above watching A Main entry.
