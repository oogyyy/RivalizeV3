# CT-Side Default for de_mirage

#### Introduction
Mirage CT-side is built around mid-control dominance through Window / Sniper's Nest. The CT team that holds Window denies Ts the ability to split either site — one AWPer in Window simultaneously covers Underpass, Catwalk, and Connector. Every rotation decision traces back to mid status. Best Window spawn wins the round's tempo — pro AWPers choose Window or Connector based purely on their spawn, never both simultaneously.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [AWPer]: Flash Short from Jungle or push Window immediately — take an aggressive wide-peek at Catwalk from Window within the first 3 seconds to catch Ts still moving, then duck back ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Support/Mid]: Throw Connector molotov from B Short or Market area landing at Connector — denies early T Connector presence and forces Ts to wait or use utility ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [B Anchor]: Molly top of B Apps from B Site to deny fast B Apps entry and gather early footstep info ▶ Watch lineup.

#### Default Positions
▸ [AWPer]: Play Window (Sniper's Nest) — covers Top Mid, Underpass, and Connector simultaneously; take one aggressive early-round peek then retreat to inside Window.
▸ [Rifler 1]: Hold Short from Ticket Booth — watches Catwalk-to-Short and can swing Palace from CT if AWPer opens mid.
▸ [Support 1]: Anchor A Site from Jungle or CT angle — crossfire partner with AWPer; holds Ramp and Short simultaneously; Jungle is a flexible position that can support mid, A, or rotate B.
▸ [Support 2]: Anchor B Site from Van or Bench — watches B Apps main choke and B Short; must be able to 1vX delay; dying early on B collapses the entire site.
▸ [IGL]: Play Market / Kitchen or CT Spawn — rotating position; monitors mid status and is first responder to either site; calls shifts from 2-1-2 to 3-1-1 based on T patterns.

#### Rotation Rules
▸ If Window AWP dies or retreats: Mid player must abandon Connector and fall back; B rotator moves toward CT Spawn to form a safety valve; full team collapses toward remaining A player faster than normal.
▸ If B Apps footsteps (multiple players heard): Market/Kitchen player commits to B immediately; AWPer rotates via CT Spawn.
▸ If Jungle + Ramp smoked simultaneously (A execute): both A players stay; IGL calls B player to CT Spawn ready for A support.
▸ If Catwalk presence confirmed: Short player peeks and delays, calls for A anchor to pre-aim Short-to-site angle.
▸ If bomb plant heard: nearest CT uses fastest route; do not double-rotate; assign one dedicated entry and one arriving 3–5 seconds later.

#### Crossfire Setups
▸ Ticket Booth + Jungle: Ticket player holds the open A Site angle; Jungle player holds the closer corner. T pushing out of A Main faces two angles separated by vertical height — nearly impossible to clear because both players see different contact points simultaneously.
▸ Window + Underpass: AWP in Window covers Top Mid catwalk approach; a rifler in Underpass covers the lower mid angle. Any T taking mid trades into two different ranges at once.
▸ Van + B Short: Van player peeks Apps entry (mid-range); Short player watches B Short approach (close-range). Ts must clear two independent angles simultaneously to take site.

#### Retake Coordination
▸ A Site Retake: Ticket/CT Spawn player enters first from CT — throws flash over Ticket to blind Triple Box / Jungle area. Jungle/Short player flanks from Short side, enters after flash. Molotov Sandwich corner to flush campers. CT player takes Ramp/Triple Box side; Short player takes Ticket/CT angle. Never all 5 rushing simultaneously.
▸ B Site Retake: Market/Kitchen player pushes through Market window or door. One CT rotates from A via CT Spawn and enters from B Short. Flash over Market to blind B Apps exit point (Bench/Van area). Molotov Van or Bench to clear post-plant campers. Do NOT chase through B Apps — retake only from CT Spawn and Market sides.

#### Adapting to T Reads
▸ If Ts are fast rushing B Apps (3+ into Apps):
- B anchor holds Van deep — do not aggress Apps corridor. IGL rotates through CT Spawn.
- AWPer drops from Window via Jungle-to-Short shortcut. Do NOT let both players die before rotating.
▸ If Ts are slow-defaulting with Mid control (Window smokes, Connector pressure):
- AWPer must win the Window duel or immediately fall back to Short — never concede both Window and Short.
- Apply early CT aggression via Jungle / Connector peeks (flash then peek) to disrupt T timing before they establish Connector.
▸ If Ts are stacking A with Palace + Ramp pressure:
- IGL calls early A rotation. AWPer drops Window immediately and assists from Ticket Booth.
- IGL smokes B Site entry from CT Spawn to prevent a last-second B redirect while A collapses.

#### Pro-Level Nuances
▸ Spawn advantage: First Window spawn wins tempo — AWPers move to Window or Connector immediately based on their spawn, not after assessing; hesitation concedes the early peek.
▸ Utility over aim on mid: Pro CT teams invest 2–3 utility pieces into mid control every round. Skipping Connector molotov is giving Ts free Connector for zero cost.
▸ Van/Bench accountability: B anchor must be able to 1vX delay — hold for trades, not die immediately. An early B anchor death collapses the entire B site.
▸ Jungle hybrid utility: Jungle is the only CT position that can simultaneously support A, support mid, or rotate to B faster than CT Spawn on short notice — use this flexibility.
