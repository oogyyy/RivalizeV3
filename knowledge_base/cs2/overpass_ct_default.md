# Overpass — CT-Side Default

**Map overview (CT-perspective):** Overpass CT wins rely on an aggressive AWP at Connector (punishing T-Short) and solid B-Main utility denial. B-Site has excellent crossfire positions. Rotations are medium-speed via the sewer/underground connector.

---

## Starting Positions

| Role | Callout | Anchor Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| AWPer | Connector (Short side) | Opens Connector duel; threatens T pushing A-Short; repositions to site |
| Entry | A-Site (Bank side) | Anchors A; crossfire with AWPer; holds Connector drop angle |
| Support | B-Site (Fountain) | Anchors B with utility; holds Fountain + Monster angles |
| Lurker | B-Site (Playground) | Second B anchor; holds Playground/CT side of B |
| IGL | CT Spawn (Sewer/underground) | Reads approach direction; rotates via Sewer or Short |

**Standard setup: 1 Connector (AWP), 1 A-Site, 2 B-Site, 1 IGL rotating**

---

## Early Map Control (1:40 → 1:10)

AWPer opens Connector at 1:42, punishing any T taking A-Short early. Support throws a molotov into B-Main chokepoint at 1:52 to slow T's early walk. Lurker holds Playground passively.

**Opening utility:**
- **Support** molotovs **B-Main** from **Fountain** at 1:52 — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+overpass+molotov+fountain+b+main+ct)
- **Lurker** throws HE into **Monster** at 1:50 to punish a fast B push — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+overpass+he+monster+ct)
- **AWPer** flashes **Connector** from **Short** at 1:42 for the peek — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+overpass+flash+short+connector+ct)

## Rotation Rules

| Trigger | Response |
|---|---|
| AWPer picks T at Connector | AWPer repositions to Short or A-Site; standard hold continues |
| Support calls "smoke + flash B-Main" | Lurker peeks Playground; IGL rotates from Sewer into B |
| Entry calls "A-Short smoke + Bank smoke" | AWPer falls back to A-Site; IGL calls "rotating A" from CT |
| B-Main smoke + Monster smoke simultaneously | Full B execute coming — IGL rotates immediately; AWPer holds A solo short-term |
| 5-man commitment confirmed one side | The other side anchors alone; no split rotation |

---

## Crossfire Setups

**Setup 1 — Connector + Short (A-Side):**
- **AWPer** at **Connector** (long range, punishes A-Short)
- **Entry** at **Bank** (close angle into A-Short exit)
- T committing A-Short faces both angles at once

**Setup 2 — Fountain + Playground (B-Site):**
- **Support** at **Fountain** (holds Monster entry)
- **Lurker** at **Playground** (holds CT-B side)
- Classic B crossfire; T entering B-Main clears Monster angle first but Playground kills the second player

**Setup 3 — Sewer + Short (Retake):**
- **IGL** enters from **Sewer** (underground)
- **AWPer** enters from **Short** (above ground)
- Two-pronged B retake; T can't cover both

---

## Retake — A Site

1. **AWPer** (at Connector) re-enters **A-Short → A-Site** after smoke clears — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+overpass+a+retake+short+side)
2. **IGL** rotates via **CT Spawn → Short** to assist
3. **Entry** re-enters from **Bank** side (if they had survived early)
4. Pop flash from Short into A-Site; AWPer + IGL retake together
5. **Lurker** stays B — do not abandon B without 100% confirmation

---

## Retake — B Site

1. **Support** holds **Fountain** (doesn't push into smokes; waits)
2. **IGL** pushes through **Sewer** into **B-Site from CT-side** — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+overpass+b+retake+sewer)
3. **AWPer** rotates from **Short → Sewer → B** OR via **Short**
4. Pop flash from Sewer exit into B-Site; IGL + Support retake together
5. Lurker watches **Playground** — catches T retreating

---

## Economy

- **CT pistol:** Support + Lurker buy armor for B (primary threat). AWPer buys P250. Entry buys HE for Connector.
- **Anti-eco:** AWP Connector; passive B hold. Kills on Connector won alone.
- **Force buy:** Coordinate all-in or all-save; never split.
