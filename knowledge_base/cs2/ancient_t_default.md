# T-Side Default for de_ancient

#### Introduction
Our T-side default on Ancient focuses on Cave / Mid (Donut) control to split CT rotational timing, while simultaneously applying B Main pressure. Winning Mid forces CTs to defend two long rotational distances and opens up clean executes on both sites.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [Support 1]: Throw a Cave smoke from T Spawn to safely enter Cave and begin Mid control without being peeked from CT Mid ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Support 2]: Throw a Donut smoke from T Mid to block CT Mid vision and allow the team to safely enter the Mid area ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Entry]: Flash Cave entry from T Spawn to help teammates push into Cave safely ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [IGL]: Molly CT Mid / the far Donut corner to clear any aggressive CT holding in that area ▶ Watch lineup.

#### Info-Gathering Positions
▸ [Lurker]: Push B Main to gauge CT numbers at B Site and create credible entry threat.
▸ [AWPer]: Hold Mid (Donut area) for early CT picks or take an aggressive Cave peek for opening information.
▸ [Support 1]: Advance through Cave to establish Mid control alongside the Entry fragger.
▸ [Entry]: Push into Donut / Mid to detect CT presence and call the split direction to the team.
▸ [IGL + Support 2]: Manage A Main approach and provide Mid information to coordinate the full team commit.

#### Default Split Routes
▸ Default A Split: Entry + AWPer + one Support commit A via A Short (through Mid) or A Main after Mid control is established. Smokes cover A Site CT corner and A Ramp angles. IGL stays in Mid to delay CT rotation.
▸ Default B Split: Entry + Lurker push B Main hard once Mid is occupied and CT attention is split. Remaining players hold Mid / Cave to prevent a fast CT B rotation. Smokes cover CT B and B Ramp entry.

#### Timing Breakpoints
▸ First 30s: Take Cave and contest Mid / Donut area. Lurker applies B Main pressure without overcommitting to the site.
▸ Mid-Round (30s-45s): If Mid control is won, force a site commit based on Lurker's B read or AWPer's A Short information.
▸ Late Round (45s+): Slow down inside Mid, use a 1v1 AWP pick opportunity, or force a late B execute with remaining utility.

#### Adapting to CT Reads
▸ If CTs are aggressive in Mid (CT Mid peek or Cave rush):
- Use double smokes in Cave and Donut. AWPer holds from T side for the counter-peek.
- Route 3 players to B Main once Mid is denied. Use B Site smokes to execute cleanly.
▸ If CTs are stacking A with 3+ players:
- 3 players commit B Main with full utility (CT B + B Ramp smokes).
- Keep 2 players maintaining Mid pressure to delay any CT rotation attempt.
