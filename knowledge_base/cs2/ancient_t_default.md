# T-Side Default for de_ancient

#### Introduction
Ancient is one of the most T-sided maps in the current pool (~52–53% T win rate at pro level) because the CT rotation from B to A takes 14+ seconds — one of the longest on any active duty map. Our default exploits this by establishing Donut (Mid) control, which simultaneously threatens both sites and forces CTs to defend reactively. The Instant Elbow smoke is thrown before any T takes a step toward mid.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [Support 1]: Throw Instant Elbow smoke (stand at right edge of wall outside Elbow, aim at mark above cracks on wall surface, jump-throw) — thrown before any mid movement; blocks CT AWPer and rifles holding Elbow / Donut entrance from CT side ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Support 2]: Throw Mid Doors smoke from T Mid Ramp to block CT vision through Mid Doors arch and allow safe T passage into Donut ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Entry]: Flash A Main long angle from T Main approach to deny CT pushing aggressively down A Main onto approaching Ts ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [IGL]: Molly CT Mid / far Donut corner immediately after Elbow smoke lands to clear any aggressive CT holding the Donut area — targets the CT most likely to delay T mid control.

#### Info-Gathering Positions
▸ [Entry]: Push A Main toward Elbow — applies A-side pressure; gathers info on CT positioning at A Main and Elbow entry; ready to contest A Main long or wait for Mid smoke to enable split.
▸ [AWPer]: Hold T Mid Ramp behind Elbow + Mid Door smokes — AWP peek through smoked Elbow once smokes are established, or wait for CT to over-aggress and counter; primary mid info gatherer.
▸ [Support 1]: Advance through Elbow into Donut after smokes land — takes Donut control alongside Entry; calls CT Donut commitment for IGL's site selection.
▸ [IGL]: Take B Main → Water / Cave approach — gathers B Cave CT positioning; reads whether CT is playing one or two in Cave; provides the B vs. A decision input.
▸ [Lurker]: Take T Mid → Donut (after smokes) — holds Donut control once established; waits as a split threat; can rotate to A via Elbow side or B via Water passage based on IGL call.

#### Default Split Routes
▸ Default A Donut Split (most common pro pattern — "2-3 Mid split"): 2 players pressure A Main while 3 control Donut via Mid. Once Donut is controlled, commit A execute — 1 enters from A Main, 1 arrives from Elbow side (Donut → Elbow → A Site), splitting two entry angles CTs cannot cover simultaneously. Smokes: Elbow, Mid Doors, Temple, CT Spawn. Molotov: under-balcony pillar on A Site.
▸ Default B Cave Execute: IGL calls B after reading single CT or no CT in Cave. Entry + Lurker hit B from B Main with Cave smoke + CT Spawn smoke + entry flash. Two remaining players hold Donut to cut the 14-second CT B-to-A rotation — if B plants before CT arrives, the round is won.

#### Timing Breakpoints
▸ First 30s: Instant Elbow smoke lands before any T advances. Mid Door smoke follows. Take Donut control — this is not optional. Lurker and IGL read their respective routes (B Main and A Main) simultaneously. Do NOT commit to a site before Donut is secured.
▸ Mid-Round (30s-45s): If Donut is controlled and AWPer forced / killed CT mid — call A execute via Elbow split (Temple + CT smokes) OR fake A molotov sounds then commit B immediately, exploiting the 14-second CT rotation window. If IGL reports single CT in Cave — call B fast execute.
▸ Late Round (45s+): Slow down inside Donut with AWPer — force a 1v1 AWP pick from Donut onto any CT peeking. Commit B with remaining utility or reset if round clock allows.

#### Adapting to CT Reads
▸ If CTs are aggressive in Mid (CT Mid peek through Elbow or Cave rush):
- Instant Elbow smoke already denies their peek. AWPer holds counter-peek from T Ramp cover.
- Double smoke Mid (Elbow + Mid Door) seals CT aggression entirely. Redirect 3 players to B Main with Cave + CT smokes while CT attention is on Mid.
▸ If CTs are stacking A with 3+ players:
- Exploit the 14-second CT rotation: commit B fast with Cave + CT Spawn smokes. Entry + Lurker hit B before A CTs can arrive.
- Keep 2 players in Donut maintaining Mid pressure to delay any CT rotation attempt.
▸ If CTs are playing aggressive down A Main toward Ts:
- Flash from A Main T side, then fall back and let Entry take the Elbow angle instead.
- 3 players control Donut while CT chases A Main; call B commit once CT is confirmed mispositioned.

#### Key Smoke Lineups (Pro Reference)
▸ Instant Elbow smoke: Right edge of wall outside Elbow — aim at mark above cracks on wall surface — jump-throw. Blocks AWPer and rifles in Donut / Elbow passage before any T moves.
▸ Temple smoke (A execute): A Main left corner, crouch — aim at top-right leaf on temple brick — W+jump throw (moving forward while jumping). Blocks CT AWP from Temple elevation above A Site.
▸ CT Spawn smoke (A execute): A Main / entry approach — aimed at CT Spawn rotation path — blocks CT rotators from A Spawn side.
▸ Cave smoke (B execute): B Main approach — aimed at Cave exit / Cave pillar — denies CT's strongest Cave anchor position; mandatory first smoke for B entry.
▸ Under-balcony molotov (A execute): Thrown from A Main at the CT-side pillar below balcony — flushes CTs hiding under balcony before Entry commits.
