# Ancient — T-Side Default

**Map overview (T-perspective):** Ancient is a map where mid control (Donut) is decisive — teams that control Donut can execute to either site with a short rotation. B-Main is slow and chokepoint-heavy; A-Main + Temple is the faster execute route. The best T defaults apply simultaneous Donut + A-Main pressure.

---

## Starting Positions

| Role | Callout | Opening Job |
|---|---|---|
| AWPer | T-Mid | Peek Donut / open mid for team; contest CT Donut control |
| Entry | T-Spawn → A-Main | Walk A-Main for early A CT information |
| Support | T-Mid (with AWPer) | Smoke Donut; flash for AWPer's mid peek |
| Lurker | T-Spawn → B-Main | Slow walk B-Main; gathers B-Side CT count |
| IGL | T-Mid OR T-Spawn → A | Calls based on mid control status and site selection |

**Default split: 2 Mid, 2 A-Main, 1 B-Main**

---

## Default Phase (1:40 → 1:00)

AWPer contests Donut at 1:42 with Support's flash. Entry walks A-Main passively (no commitment, just information). Lurker slow-walks B-Main and reports CT aggression timing.

## Economy Considerations

- **Pistol T:** 4 rush A-Main together; 1 lurk mid. Execute A at 0:50.
- **Anti-Eco:** Rush B-Main with 4; fast A-Main pressure with 1.
- **Force Buy:** Rush A-Main — short execute distance makes force buys viable on A.
