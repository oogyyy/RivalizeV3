# T-Side Default for de_mirage

#### Introduction
Our T-side default on Mirage focuses on early Mid control, information gathering, and flexibility to react to CT setups. We use utility to take space safely before committing to a site.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [Support 1]: Throw a Window smoke from T Spawn / Mid to block CT vision from Sniper's Nest ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Support 2]: Throw a Top Mid smoke from T Spawn to secure Mid boxes and limit CT aggression from Connector ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Entry]: Flash Mid from T Mid to help the team take space safely ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [IGL/Support]: Molly Connector or Jungle if CTs are playing aggressively in Mid.

#### Info-Gathering Positions
▸ [Lurker]: Hold Palace to watch A Ramp / Balcony and detect early CT rotations.
▸ [AWPer]: Play aggressive Mid (behind smokes) or Palace for long range picks.
▸ [Support]: Take B Apartments (Apps) to monitor B site and possible CT rotations through Underpass.
▸ [Entry + Support]: Push Mid / Short to contest control and gather live information on CT positioning.

#### Default Split Routes
▸ Default A Split: Entry, one Support, and AWPer push through Mid + Short + Palace toward A site. Lurker fakes B or holds Mid. IGL provides utility support from Top Mid.
▸ Default B Split: Entry + Support execute through B Apartments with B Apps smoke and B Window smoke. The rest of the team maintains Mid pressure to delay CT rotations.

#### Timing Breakpoints
▸ First 30s: Focus on taking Mid control and gathering information. Avoid unnecessary fights without utility.
▸ Mid-Round (30s-45s): Use gathered info to decide on a site commit, fake, or reset.
▸ Late Round (45s+): Slow down, look for picks, or set up a late execute with remaining utility.

#### Adapting to CT Reads
▸ If CTs are aggressive in Mid (Connector peek / Short rush):
- Use strong Window + Top Mid smokes to stall them.
- Lurker ready to trade from Palace. Regroup and redirect if needed.
▸ If CTs are playing passively / stacked on a site:
- Take more map control (Palace + Connector).
- Prepare a coordinated A or B execute with proper site smokes (CT, Stairs, Van) and post-plant utility.
