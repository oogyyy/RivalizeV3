# T-Side Default for de_mirage

#### Introduction
Our T-side default on Mirage is built around denying the CT AWP in Window (Sniper's Nest) within the first 5 seconds, then using Mid control to split CTs between a Connector-supported A execute and a B Apartments execute. Without the Window smoke, a single CT AWPer simultaneously covers Underpass, Catwalk, and Connector — killing T-side mid-structure entirely.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [Support 1]: Throw Window smoke from T Spawn (stand near left fence / trash bin, aim top-right corner of Window door frame, jump-throw) to block CT AWPer in Sniper's Nest — first grenade of the round, every round ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Support 2]: Throw Top Mid smoke from T Spawn / Underpass position to block CT Connector aggression and secure Mid boxes safely ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Entry]: Flash Mid from T Mid (pop-flash overhand) to blind any CT aggressively peeking before Window smoke lands ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [IGL]: Molly Connector or Jungle approach if CTs are known to play aggressive early Mid — prevents CT from contesting before smokes are in place.

#### Info-Gathering Positions
▸ [Entry]: Push T Ramp toward A Short — apply A-side pressure and report CT numbers at Ticket Booth and Short.
▸ [AWPer]: Hold Top Mid behind the Window smoke — duel the CT exiting Window after the smoke lands, or wait for a cross-map pick. Underpass is only the safe rotation route up to Top Mid: from INSIDE Underpass there is NO line of sight to Window, so the hold must happen at the Top Mid / Underpass-exit level. Do NOT sit in open mid.
▸ [Support]: Rotate to B Apartments (Upper or Lower Apps) — monitor B site through Market Window and report CT B numbers before IGL commits.
▸ [IGL]: Hold B Apartments (Lower Apps or Bench) — reads CT positioning from Apartments approach; primary decision-maker for B vs. A call.
▸ [Lurker]: Take Palace toward A Ramp / Balcony — watches for CT rotating off B toward A Short; holds as late-round A split threat.

#### Default Split Routes
▸ Default A Connector Split (most common pro pattern): Entry + AWPer + one Support push through Mid (after Window smoke) → Connector → Short → A Site simultaneously with Lurker from Palace. Smokes cover CT Spawn cross, Stairs, and Jungle. IGL provides Catwalk flash support from Top Mid.
▸ Default B Apartments Execute: IGL + Support execute through B Apartments with Market Window smoke + B Short smoke. Remaining three players maintain Mid pressure (Window smoke up, AWPer at Top Mid) to delay CT rotation from A Short or CT Spawn.

#### Timing Breakpoints
▸ First 30s: Window smoke in immediately; take Mid control. Entry applies Ramp pressure; IGL reads Apartments. Do not commit to either site without information from at least two routes.
▸ Mid-Round (30s-45s): If Connector is open and AWPer gets a pick or forces a CT back — call A split. If Apartments approach is passive and B numbers are low — execute B. If no clear read, hold Mid and cycle a lurk from Palace.
▸ Late Round (45s+): Force a late A execute via Connector with AWPer entry, or set up a B execute from Apartments with whatever utility remains. Avoid unnecessary 1v1 duels in open Mid.

#### Adapting to CT Reads
▸ If CTs are aggressive in Mid (early Connector peek or Short rush):
- Window + Top Mid smokes already up — AWPer counter-peeks from behind smoke cover.
- Lurker ready to trade from Palace. Redirect 3 players to B Apartments while CT attention is on Mid.
▸ If CTs are stacking A with Palace + Ramp setup (3+ on A):
- Force a B Apartments execute immediately — Market Window smoke + Short flash + push Apps.
- IGL rotates from Apartments; AWPer holds Mid to delay any CT A-to-B rotation.
▸ If CTs are passive (no aggression on Mid or Ramp):
- Take full Connector control. Set up the Connector → Short → A split with CT Spawn, Stairs, and Jungle smokes.
- Lurker commits Palace simultaneously — T push through two different angles at once.

#### Key Smoke Lineups (Pro Reference)
▸ Window smoke: T Spawn near left fence / trash bin — aim top-right corner of Window door frame — jump-throw.
▸ A Execute CT cross smoke: Stand at Underpass ledge (center-left) — aim at left side of roof beyond A cross — jump-throw. Blocks CT from T Long to A Site crossing.
▸ A Execute Stairs smoke: T Ramp (first step corner) — left-click standing — lands on Stairs position at A Site.
▸ A Execute Jungle smoke: T Ramp (top of stairs, center of window gap) — left-click standing — blocks Jungle / CT rotation.
▸ B Execute Market Window smoke: B Apartments upper area / Van exit corner — standing throw at specific rooftop edge — cuts CT Window sight line into B Site.
