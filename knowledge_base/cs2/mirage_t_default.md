# Mirage — T-Side Default

**Map overview (T-perspective):** Mirage is a 3-lane map. T-side wins by controlling mid (Window/Connector) to enable short rotations, or by committing a coordinated 5-man execute to A or B. The team that wins mid has the fastest access to both sites and the highest execute success rate.

---

## Starting Positions

| Role | Callout | Opening Job |
|---|---|---|
| AWPer | T-Ramp (top of ramp toward Short) | Peek Short or hold Ramp for early info |
| Entry | T-Spawn → B-Apps | Fast B-Apps walk to gather B-Main info |
| Support | T-Spawn → Mid (Jungle side) | Smoke top-mid / throw into Window from Jungle |
| Lurker | T-Spawn → Short (Catwalk to Connector) | Aggressive Connector push or passive Short hold |
| IGL | T-Spawn → Ramp (with AWPer) | Gathers Mid + A info from Ramp |

---

## Default Phase (1:40 → 1:00)

The team splits control across all three lanes simultaneously to gather CT position information before committing. Support smokes top-mid from Jungle to safely transfer mid control to the Lurker. AWPer peeks Short or holds Ramp ready to punish an early CT AWP.

**Objective:** By 1:00, the team should know whether CT is stacking A (heard Ramp/Palace noise), stacking B (heard B Main utility), or playing standard (nothing heard = normal setup).

**Utility in this phase:**
- **Support** smokes **Top Mid** from **Jungle** at 1:42 — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+smoke+jungle+to+top+mid)
- **Support** can optionally flash **Window** from **Jungle** at 1:38 to help Lurker take Connector — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+flash+jungle+to+window)
- **Entry** may throw an HE grenade into **B-Apps** at 1:45 to deny early CT aggression — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+he+b+apps)

---

## Mid-Round Reads (1:00 → 0:55)

| Information | Call |
|---|---|
| AWPer gets Short pick OR Lurker takes Connector | "Short control — call A short" |
| Entry reports only 1 CT at B, IGL hears no A rotation | "Hit B — B execute" |
| No info from either site, T has mid control | "Default A — Ramp + Palace execute" |
| CT rotates heard through CT Spawn | "Fake B — redirect A" |

**IGL rule:** If no call is made by 0:55, the team defaults to **A execute via Ramp + Palace** using pre-prepared utility.

## A Execute (Ramp + Palace — "No-Info Execute")

**Trigger:** No clear information by 0:55, or AWPer confirms 1 CT at Short.

### Utility Sequence

| 2 | Support | Smoke | T-Ramp | Stairs | 0:57 |
| 3 | IGL | Smoke | Ramp | Ticket Booth | 0:56 |
| 4 | AWPer | Flash | Short | A-Site (over top) | 0:53 |
| 5 | Entry | Molotov | Palace | Default A-Plant | 0:51 |

- [▶ CT Smoke from Jungle](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+smoke+jungle+to+ct)
- [▶ Stairs Smoke from Ramp](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+smoke+ramp+to+stairs)
- [▶ Ticket Booth Smoke from Ramp](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+smoke+ramp+to+ticket+booth)
- [▶ Pop Flash over A Site](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+flash+short+over+a+site)
- [▶ Default Plant Molotov from Palace](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+molotov+palace+a+site)

### Entry & Trade Plan
1. CT smoke, Stairs smoke, and Ticket Booth smoke all up simultaneously (0:55)
2. **Entry** pushes through Ramp into A Site, clearing Stairs angle first
3. **IGL** pushes from Palace, clears CT corner and Jungle angle
4. **AWPer** pops flash and follows Entry through Ramp
5. **Lurker** stays at Connector — cuts off CT rotation via Short
6. **Support** trails, cleans Ticket Booth, plants at Default position

### Post-Plant
- **Lurker** → Connector / Short (cuts CT rotation)
- **AWPer** → Jungle (crossfire into CT side)
- **Entry** → Stairs (close hold, counters Short retake)
- **IGL** → Ramp (crossfire Stairs with Entry)
- **Support** → Ticket Booth (time-waste position, watches CT)

### Abort
If CT is heard pushing Short aggressively before smokes are up, Entry calls "abort" at 1:00 — team falls back to Mid and IGL calls a B execute.

## B Execute (B-Apps + Van Smoke)

**Trigger:** Entry confirms only 1 CT at B, or Lurker cuts off CT rotation and calls "no rotation."

### Utility Sequence
| Step | Role | Utility | From | Lands At | Clock |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Support | Smoke | T-Spawn (angled) | Van | 0:55 |
| 2 | Support | Smoke | B-Apps | CT (B-Site CT angle) | 0:54 |
| 3 | IGL | Flash | B-Apps | B-Site (pop flash) | 0:52 |
| 4 | AWPer | Molotov | B-Apps | B-Site platform | 0:50 |

- [▶ Van Smoke](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+smoke+t+spawn+to+van)
- [▶ CT Smoke B-Site](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+smoke+b+apps+to+ct)
- [▶ B Site Pop Flash](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+flash+b+apps+pop)
- [▶ Platform Molotov](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+mirage+molotov+b+apps+to+platform)

### Entry & Trade Plan
1. Van smoke up, CT smoke up (0:53)
2. **Entry** pushes through B-Apps under flash at 0:51
3. **IGL** follows Entry, clears short side of B-Site
4. **AWPer** holds B-Apps opening for any CT peeking back through smoke
5. **Support** plants at Short side (safer plant; far from CT window)
6. **Lurker** applies pressure at Short / Connector to prevent CT crossing to B

### Post-Plant
- **Entry** → B-Site close (behind boxes, counters short-rush defuse)
- **IGL** → B-Apps entrance (watching Van angle)
- **AWPer** → Van smoke line (crossfire into site through clearing smoke)
- **Support** → B-Site back (time-waste, watching CT)
- **Lurker** → Short / Connector (full map coverage)

### Abort
If CT is heard rotating through CT Spawn before 1:00, call "abort B — redirect A." Entry should smoke Van anyway to sell the fake and delay CT's awareness.

## Mid-Control Play (Alternative Opening)

If mid control is a priority (opponent's CT is AWP-camping Short or Window):

1. **Support** smokes **Top Mid** from Jungle at 1:42
2. **Lurker** pushes through Connector with Support's Window flash
3. **AWPer** holds Short to punish any CT peeking from Window after smoke
4. **Entry** and **IGL** hold Ramp / Palace passively for info
5. Once Lurker is in Connector, the team can split: Lurker hits Short into A, while Entry + IGL push Ramp

**Key advantage:** Mid control forces the CT to commit 2 players (one Short, one Window/Jungle), stretching their 5-man setup.

## Economy Considerations

- **Pistol T (Round 1):** All 5 push together toward B-Apps; Entry + IGL buy armor; Support buys HE for B-Apps denial; AWPer buys Deagle or P250. Execute B on pistol.
- **Anti-Eco (after winning Round 1):** 3 players rifle up; 2 players buy SMGs/Galils. Push fast B before CT can buy rifles from the pistol win.
- **Force Buy:** If team bank is $3,500–$4,000 per player: coordinated AK + no armor; Support drops armor for utility instead.
