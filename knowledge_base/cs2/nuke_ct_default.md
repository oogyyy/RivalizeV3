# CT-Side Default for de_nuke

#### Introduction
Our CT-side default on Nuke is built around Heaven AWP dominance, solid Upper anchoring, and a two-player Lower hold to deny Secret entry. The IGL reads T Outside movement to determine which site is the primary target and calls rotations accordingly.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [AWPer]: Flash from Heaven position toward Outside to punish early T Ramp push ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Lower Anchor]: Molly Secret entry from Lower Site to slow down T Lurker commitment via Secret ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [Upper Anchor]: Flash Outside from Ramp door position to punish any immediate T rush through Ramp ▶ Watch lineup.

#### Default Positions
▸ [AWPer]: Play Heaven — punish T Outside movement and maintain Ramp entry threat from above.
▸ [Rifler 1]: Anchor Upper Site from Trophy / Hut position, holding Ramp entry angle.
▸ [Rifler 2]: Anchor Upper Site from Rafters / Vents position as secondary support and rotation blocker.
▸ [Support 1]: Anchor Lower Site — hold Vents drop and Secret entry. Call T Lower commitment early.
▸ [IGL]: Play Lower Site / Sewer area — read T Outside commitment direction and coordinate Upper/Lower rotation.

#### Rotation Rules
▸ If Ramp is smoked (Upper execute incoming): AWPer stays Heaven as long as possible, then falls back to Upper Site. IGL rotates Upper via Sewer.
▸ If Secret is heavily committed (Lower execute): IGL + Lower anchor hold Lower. AWPer drops from Heaven and clears Vents to assist Upper retake from outside.
▸ If Outside is controlled but T has not committed yet: AWPer opens Heaven duels. Upper anchors stay passive and wait.
▸ If bomb is planted Upper: Lower anchor rotates through Sewer / Vents. Flash Upper Site from Vents entry.

#### Crossfire Setups
▸ Heaven + Ramp: AWPer holds Ramp entry from Heaven above; rifler holds from Trophy below. T entering Upper faces a vertical crossfire — one angle is unreachable by a single flash.
▸ Lower Vents + Secret: One player holds the Vent drop (close); the other holds from CT side of Lower (long range). T dropping Vents is caught mid-air or on landing.
▸ Upper + Hut: Two Upper anchors — one at Hut and one at Trophy — create a lateral crossfire across Upper Site.

#### Retake Coordination
▸ Upper Site Retake: AWPer from Heaven; Rifler 1 from outside Ramp re-entry; IGL through Sewer to Vents. Flash Upper from Vents entry. Clear Trophy first, then commit.
▸ Lower Site Retake: Upper anchors drop through Vents. IGL + Lower player converge from both sides. Flash from Vents entry into Lower. Clear back of Lower first, then Squeaky Door side.

#### Adapting to T Reads
▸ If Ts are rushing Ramp with 4+ players:
- AWPer opens Heaven angle immediately — do not delay. Rifler 1 plays deep Hut, not aggressive Ramp.
- Lower anchor stays Lower — do not pull for Upper unless T commitment to Upper is fully confirmed.
▸ If Ts split Outside + Secret simultaneously:
- Do not rotate too early. Keep 2 Upper, 2 Lower, and AWPer in Heaven until the primary threat is confirmed.
- IGL calls which side is the primary after hearing both Lurker's Secret sound and Entry's Outside commitment.
