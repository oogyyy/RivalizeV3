# Nuke — CT-Side Default

**Map overview (CT-perspective):** Nuke is CT-sided because the AWP at Heaven/Roof punishes T's Outside approach, and fast rotations between Upper and Lower are available via the internal connector (Squeaky Door, Vent, Ramp). The key is maintaining Outside pressure, using the AWP aggressively, and not surrendering Ramp control.

---

## Starting Positions

| Role | Callout | Anchor Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| AWPer | Heaven (or Roof Outside) | Controls Outside; denies T map control; rotates via Ramp |
| Entry | Outside (Hut side) | Peeks T attempting Outside; provides early Outside info |
| Support | A-Site (Upper) | Anchors Upper A; holds Ramp angle with crossfire; assists AWPer |
| Lurker | B-Site (Lower) | Anchors Lower; calls Secret approach; holds Squeaky/Vent |
| IGL | CT Spawn (connector) | Reads T approach direction; rotates to whichever site needs help |

**Standard setup: AWP Heaven, 1 Outside, 1 Upper A, 1 Lower B, 1 IGL rotating**

---

## Early Map Control (1:40 → 1:10)

AWPer at Heaven opens Outside at 1:43 — the Heaven AWP is Nuke's most impactful position. Entry peeks Hut to catch T trying to push. Support holds Ramp from A-Site. Lurker throws an HE into Secret at 1:50 to damage a T taking the Secret route.

## Rotation Rules

| Trigger | Response |
|---|---|
| Entry calls "T Hut control" | AWPer drops from Heaven to Ramp top; Entry falls back to Outside Alley |
| Lurker calls "smoke Secret + flash Secret" | IGL pushes from CT → Squeaky to reinforce Lower |
| Support calls "Lobby smoke + Ramp smoke" | AWPer already on Ramp; Entry rotates from Outside toward Lobby; IGL holds CT |
| Both Upper and Lower being hit simultaneously | Split situation — AWPer holds Upper, Lurker holds Lower, IGL stays connector |
| Lurker calls "vent entry" confirmed | IGL pushes Vent from CT side to cut off entry |

## Crossfire Setups

**Setup 1 — Heaven + Ramp (Upper A):**
- **AWPer** holds from **Heaven** (long-range angle toward Ramp entry)
- **Support** holds from **Ramp-top position** in Upper A
- T must win a Heaven AWP duel before safely pushing Ramp; impossible to peek both simultaneously

**Setup 2 — Squeaky Door + Hell (Lower B):**
- **Lurker** holds **Squeaky Door** side
- **IGL** holds **Hell exit**
- T entering from Secret must clear both angles; classic Lower crossfire

**Setup 3 — Outside + Hut (Outside control):**
- **AWPer** holds from **Outside Alley / Garage**
- **Entry** holds from **Hut**
- Maintains Outside map control; T cannot walk freely

## Retake — Upper A

**Scenario:** T executes Ramp with Heaven smoke, Trophy smoke, Control Room smoke.

1. Wait for Heaven smoke to clear — **AWPer** pushes from **Heaven** once smoke dissipates
2. **Support** re-enters from **CT Spawn → Ramp** — pops flash into site — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+nuke+flash+ramp+upper+retake)
3. **Entry** rotates from Outside through Lobby → Ramp
4. AWPer + Support retake together; Entry covers the Ramp exit below
5. **IGL** pushes from **Squeaky** to catch any T holding Lower connector

---

## Retake — Lower B

**Scenario:** T executes Lower with Radio smoke and Hell smoke.

1. **Lurker** already on Lower — pops flash from **B back corner** — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+nuke+lower+retake+flash)
2. **IGL** pushes through **Squeaky Door** into Lower; enters from the back angle
3. **AWPer** rotates from Heaven through **Ramp → Vent → Lower** (slower but impactful)
4. Lurker + IGL retake simultaneously; AWPer provides AWP crossfire through Vent
5. **Support** holds Upper — do not abandon Upper unless all T confirmed Lower

## Economy

- **CT pistol:** Lurker + IGL buy armor for B defense. AWPer buys P250. Outside Entry buys HE. Support buys HE for Ramp denial.
- **Anti-eco:** AWP full buys; play Heaven passively. Outside Entry plays very safely — one player with an AWP from Heaven wins anti-eco rounds alone.
- **After CT force buy:** Play extremely passive; use the AWP position as the entire economy strategy. One pick from Heaven can win a round even with pistols elsewhere.
