# CT-Side Default for de_inferno

#### Introduction
Our CT-side default on Inferno divides two players on A (Pit and Library), two on B (Balcony and CT), and an IGL roaming to read Banana and Apartments aggression. Utility is used on both sides early to slow T execution timing and maintain information control.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [B Anchor 1]: Molly top of Banana from B Balcony to deny T early Banana push and gather sound info ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [A Anchor 1]: Flash Arch entry from Library position to punish T Apartment aggression ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [IGL/Support]: Smoke Second / mid-Banana from CT area to delay T Banana timing when needed ▶ Watch lineup.

#### Default Positions
▸ [AWPer]: Play Pit (A Site) or B Balcony — take early picks on T Apartments approach or T Banana aggression.
▸ [Rifler 1]: Anchor A from Library, holding CT App entry and clearing Graveyard angle.
▸ [Rifler 2]: Anchor B from CT position (CT spawn side of B), holding Coffin and Balcony crossfire.
▸ [Support]: Play B Balcony — deny T early Second control and provide fire support on Banana.
▸ [IGL]: Roam Arch / Mid to gather Apartments + Banana information and call rotations based on both sides.

#### Rotation Rules
▸ If Banana is heavily smoked (full B execute incoming): AWPer pivots from Pit to B via CT Spawn. IGL rotates B. A anchor holds solo.
▸ If Apartments are being aggressively pushed: IGL rushes to Library to assist. B anchors stay patient on B.
▸ If Library + Graveyard + CT are all smoked (A execute): all CT players rotate A. B Balcony player delays Banana with remaining utility before rotating.
▸ If bomb is planted on B: Rifler 1 rotates from A via CT Spawn. AWPer holds Arch to cut any T flank.

#### Crossfire Setups
▸ B Balcony + CT: One holds Balcony (close-range B entry); the other holds CT angle (longer range). T entering B is split between two angles that cannot be cleared with a single flash.
▸ Pit + Library: AWPer in Pit holds A Site long angle; rifler in Library holds CT App entry and Graveyard. T executing A faces both simultaneously.
▸ Arch + B Balcony: IGL holds Arch mid-range while B Balcony player watches Banana. Ts trying a split are caught crossing between the two.

#### Retake Coordination
▸ A Site Retake: AWPer from Pit; rifler from Library. IGL rotates from Arch. Flash into A Site. Clear Graveyard first, then commit to the site with numbers.
▸ B Site Retake: B anchors hold close (do not push into Banana smoke). IGL + rotating A rifler enter from CT Spawn. Flash B Site from CT. Clear Coffin first, then Balcony / boxes.

#### Adapting to T Reads
▸ If Ts fast rush Banana with 3+ players:
- B anchors play extremely passive (deep B Site — do not peek Banana at all).
- IGL rotates B immediately. A players hold solo and delay.
▸ If Ts are slow Apartment default with 3 on A side:
- IGL aggressively flashes Arch and peeks to slow their progress.
- B anchors can pinch through CT Spawn to assist A if confident that B is clear.
