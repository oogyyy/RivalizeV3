# Inferno — CT-Side Default

**Map overview (CT-perspective):** CTs win Inferno by controlling Banana with utility (molotovs deny fast pushes) and keeping the Second Mid AWP alive for information. A-side is anchored by 2 CTs using an Apartments + Arch crossfire. Rotations should be slow and deliberate — Inferno is a CT-sided map precisely because rotations are fast via CT Spawn.

---

## Starting Positions

| Role | Callout | Anchor Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| AWPer | Second Mid (Alt Mid / Banana entry) | Opens Second Mid duel; denies T Banana control |
| Entry | A-Site (pit / Arch side) | Anchors A with Support; holds Apartments drop angle |
| Support | A-Site (Apartments upper / CT corner) | Crossfire with Entry; throws opening molotovs; holds Apartments |
| Lurker | B-Site (Coffin / back of B) | Anchors B; calls Banana push timing; holds van/coffin |
| IGL | B-Site (Banana side) OR CT Spawn | Calls B timing; rotates quickly via CT Spawn |

**Standard setup: 2 A (Entry + Support), 1 Second Mid (AWP), 2 B (IGL + Lurker)**

---

## Early Map Control (1:40 → 1:10)

AWPer opens Second Mid at 1:43 to contest T Banana push. Lurker throws a molotov at the top of Banana (First Banana) at 1:52 to slow T approach. Support can throw an HE up the Apartments stairs to punish a fast T run through.

**Opening utility:**
- **Lurker** molotovs **Top Banana** (first corner) from **B-Site** at 1:52 — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+inferno+molotov+b+site+top+banana+ct)

## Rotation Rules

| Trigger | Response |
|---|---|
| AWPer picks T at Second Banana | AWPer falls back to Coffin or CT Spawn; Lurker holds B solo short-term |
| Lurker calls "molotov + flash Banana" | IGL rotates from CT Spawn to B; Lurker holds Coffin; do not rotate A yet |
| Entry calls "Apartments smoke + Arch smoke simultaneously" | AWPer rotates from Second Mid to Short (A-side); IGL holds B with Lurker |
| Support calls "2 T Apartments, 2 T Arch approaching" | AWPer immediately calls "rotating A" and enters via CT; IGL holds B alone until backup available |
| Both sites getting smoked simultaneously | It is a split — IGL calls "hold, no rotate" and both A and B anchor independently |

## Crossfire Setups

**Setup 1 — Second Mid + Balcony (B-Side):**
- **AWPer** holds from **Second Mid** (open Banana duel)
- **Lurker** holds from **Balcony/Van** (catches T pushing under AWPer's smoke)
- T must win a long-range AWP duel or smoke Second Mid; either way is costly

**Setup 2 — Arch + CT corner (A-Site):**
- **Entry** holds from **Arch** (angle into Apartments drop)
- **Support** holds from **CT corner** (angle into Arch/Long side)
- T entering from Apartments must clear both simultaneously

**Setup 3 — Coffin + Back-site (B-Site):**
- **IGL** holds **Coffin** (close range, behind boxes)
- **Lurker** holds **B-Site back** (behind boiler or dark corner)
- Makes a rushed B execute extremely difficult to clean up quickly

## Retake — A Site

**Scenario:** T executes via Apartments with CT smoke, Graveyard smoke, Library smoke.

1. **AWPer** pushes from **CT Spawn → Short → Arch** — fastest A retake route — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+inferno+a+retake+short+arch)
2. **Support** re-enters from **Apartments** (re-climb or drop back from CT); clears far CT side
3. **Entry** pops flash from **Arch** into A-Site; enters from Short side
4. Wait for library smoke to clear (or throw a flash into library) before pushing that angle
5. **IGL** rotates from B (only if B is safe) via CT Spawn; defuses from near Graveyard

---

## Retake — B Site

**Scenario:** T executes B Banana with Coffin smoke and CT-back smoke.

1. **Lurker** already on B — pops flash from **B-Site back corner** (smoke line) and pushes in
2. **IGL** (CT Spawn) rotates fast — enters from **CT → Banana** — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+inferno+b+retake+ct+banana)
3. **AWPer** rotates from Second Mid through CT Spawn to Banana exit to cut off T AWPer angle
4. IGL + Lurker enter B simultaneously from opposite angles; AWPer provides crossfire from Banana
5. **Entry** stays A — do not abandon A on B retake unless confirmed all 5 T on B

## Economy

- **CT pistol:** Lurker + IGL buy armor + P250 for B. Entry + Support buy HE for Apartments. AWPer buys P250.
- **Anti-eco:** AWPer full buys; hold B and A passively — Banana molotovs on eco rounds stop rushes cold.
- **After 3 consecutive losses:** Full team buy or full save — no mixed buy states.
