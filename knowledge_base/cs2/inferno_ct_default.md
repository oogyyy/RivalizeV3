# CT-Side Default for de_inferno

#### Introduction
Inferno CT-side is decided by Banana control and Apartments denial. Banana is the only T-side path to B — losing it unpunished gives Ts a free B plant. The core defensive principle is non-negotiable: invest 2 utility pieces into Banana at round start every single round. A site is defended through the Pit / Truck crossfire, which makes clearing A nearly impossible without coordinated T utility. Note: the 2025 update widened Banana, barred Graveyard, and extended Balcony — Balcony is now a primary AWP position and the Graveyard hold is no longer viable.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [B Anchor 1]: Throw CT-side Banana molotov from Second Oranges or CT B area landing at Top of Banana / First Oranges — delays T Banana push by 3–4 seconds; forces Ts to wait for it to burn or push through fire damage ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [B Anchor 2]: Pop CT flash over the Banana wall immediately after molotov lands — blinds any T at Top of Banana who waited for molotov to clear; the molotov + flash combo is the standard pro Banana investment ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [IGL/Support]: Throw Boiler / Arch molotov from Arch area landing at Boiler passage to slow T lurkers pushing through Boiler toward mid ▶ Watch lineup.

#### Default Positions
▸ [AWPer]: Play Truck or Balcony (A Site) — Balcony is now a primary position after the 2025 update extending it; watches A Short, A Main entry, and the Cemetery crossing.
▸ [Rifler 1]: Anchor A from Pit — one of the strongest single 1v1 positions in CS2; watches Cemetery/Balcony approach, A Short exit, and has a fast escape route; never leave Pit empty.
▸ [Rifler 2]: Anchor B from Second Oranges or CT B — watches mid-Banana choke and B Site entry; takes the aggressive Second Oranges early-round peek into Banana.
▸ [Support]: Hold Coffins or Dark — watches the final step onto B Site after Ts win Banana; Coffins off-angle catches T demo-prepared players who expect a standard hold.
▸ [IGL]: Roam Arch / Library — gathers Apartments + Banana information simultaneously; first responder for A execute and rotation caller for Banana commitments.

#### Rotation Rules
▸ If Banana molotov burns out and flashbang heard (B execute likely): both B anchors tighten positions — no forward Banana hold; IGL begins rotating from Arch toward B via CT Spawn; A player starts rotation at plant confirmation.
▸ If Apartments footsteps heard in Boiler: Arch / IGL player alerts A Site; second B player may drop toward Arch to intercept.
▸ If A Short peek confirmed: AWP on Truck / Balcony engages immediately; Pit player holds to form Pit + Truck crossfire.
▸ If bomb plant on B confirmed: A players rotate via CT Spawn; arrive 5–7 seconds post-plant; IGL can flash ahead of rotation.
▸ If bomb plant on A confirmed: B players rotate through Arch (5–7 seconds); IGL pops flash ahead of their entry.

#### Crossfire Setups
▸ Pit + Truck / Balcony (A Site): Pit player watches left Cemetery approach; Truck / Balcony player watches right / Short exit. T pushing Short or from Apartments faces both simultaneously — the Pit player is nearly impossible to spot before being hit due to the deeply recessed angle.
▸ Second Oranges + Coffins (B Site): Second Oranges player watches mid-Banana choke; Coffins player watches the final step onto site from an off-angle. A T winning Banana must still locate and kill the Coffins off-angle CT.
▸ Arch + Library (post-plant ambush): Arch player holds the CT-to-B rotation corridor; Library player holds the A-to-Arch sightline. Any T rotating mid-round without information is caught between two ambush positions.

#### Retake Coordination
▸ A Site Retake: Pit player (if alive) delays Ts from planting — forces T exposure before retake team arrives. Library / Arch player enters from Library side, throws flash into site over left-side wall to blind Truck and CT-facing angles. CT Spawn player enters from A Short with a second flash. Molotov the CT-facing plant spot (behind Truck) to force Ts off. Arch/Library player clears left side (Pit corner, Cemetery); Short player clears right side (Truck, CT area).
▸ B Site Retake: CT side player enters B from Arch corridor — flash thrown into site over the Arch wall to blind Coffins / Dark positions. Molotov New Box or Second Oranges post-plant position. A-side player rotates through CT and enters from B Short (behind Coffins) as the second angle. Never retake through Banana — too slow and exposes you to the bomb timer.

#### Adapting to T Reads
▸ If Ts fast rush Banana with 3+ players:
- Both B anchors play extremely passive (deep B Site — absolutely do NOT peek Banana).
- IGL rotates B immediately from Arch. A players hold solo and delay with utility.
- Amateur mistake: peeking Banana during a rush — CTs die and the entire B defense collapses.
▸ If Ts are running slow Apartment default with 3 on A side:
- IGL aggressively flashes Arch then peeks through Boiler to slow Apartment timing.
- B anchors can pinch through CT Spawn to assist A only if B is confirmed clear — do not leave B empty on a read.
▸ If Ts fake Banana then execute A:
- Banana molotov + flash already invested — CT is not caught off-guard if Banana utility fires.
- Pit player delays A entry solo; Library player holds until IGL arrives via Arch rotation.

#### Pro-Level Nuances
▸ Banana is non-negotiable: Pro CTs invest 2 utility pieces into Banana at round start — always. Amateur CTs skip it and give Ts free B control. Banana utility failure is a preparation failure, not an aim failure.
▸ Pit is the map's most important 1v1 position: Pro teams never leave Pit empty — an unoccupied Pit makes A Site trivially easy to execute into.
▸ 2025 Balcony extension: Balcony is now a primary AWP position that wasn't viable before the update — pro AWPers increasingly use Balcony over Truck for the wider A Short angle.
▸ 2025 Graveyard barred: The Graveyard hold was patched; the angle no longer exists as a standard hold. Do not attempt to play old Graveyard positions.
▸ Coffins off-angle: Play the unexpected side of Coffins instead of the standard forward-facing angle — catches T demo-prepared players who expect a fixed hold.
▸ Budget 2 mollies + 1 HE for Banana: Pro teams run this exact utility budget for B every round. Running 0 utility on Banana = a free B rush every time.
