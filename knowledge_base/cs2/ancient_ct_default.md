# CT-Side Default for de_ancient

#### Introduction
Our CT-side default on Ancient uses a 2-2-1 split: two on A, two on B, and an IGL holding Mid to block T Cave aggression and call rotations. Mid denial is critical — allowing Ts to control Donut gives them free execution options on both sites.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [IGL/Mid]: Molly Cave entrance from CT Mid to slow T Cave push and force them to use extra utility ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [A Anchor]: Flash A Main entry from A Site to punish an early T A Main rush ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [B Anchor]: Molly B Main entrance from B Site to deny a fast B rush ▶ Watch lineup.

#### Default Positions
▸ [AWPer]: Play A Site CT / Steps — punish T pushing A Short or A Main early with a long-range pick.
▸ [Rifler 1]: Anchor A Site from A Ramp side, holding entry angles from A Main.
▸ [Rifler 2]: Anchor B Site from CT B position, holding B Main entry.
▸ [Support]: Hold B Site from the B Ramp / elevated side, providing crossfire on B Main.
▸ [IGL]: Play CT Mid / Donut area to deny T Cave control and rotate quickly to either site.

#### Rotation Rules
▸ If Cave is smoked (T entering Mid): IGL falls back toward CT Spawn; calls site-side reads. AWPer holds A Short for any follow-up Mid pressure.
▸ If B Main is heavily committed (3+ heard or seen): IGL rotates B immediately. AWPer pivots A Short to CT rotation.
▸ If A Main + A Short are both smoked simultaneously: full A execute — all CTs collapse A. B anchor delays with utility.
▸ If bomb is planted B: AWPer rotates via CT Spawn. IGL from Mid enters B from CT angle.

#### Crossfire Setups
▸ A Site + A Short: AWPer holds from deep A Site (or Steps); rifler holds A Short entry — T committed to A Main faces both angles at once.
▸ B Main + B Ramp: Rifler at B Ramp (elevated) watches B Main entry; second anchor holds CT B flat. T pushing B must clear both vertical levels.
▸ CT Mid + A Short: IGL holds Donut / CT Mid while AWPer covers A Short — T controlling Mid is threatened from two directions.

#### Retake Coordination
▸ A Site Retake: AWPer from A Short; rifler from A Ramp. IGL rotates from Mid. Flash into A Site from A Short. Clear Steps first, then commit.
▸ B Site Retake: B anchor holds close. IGL + AWPer rotate via CT Spawn. Flash from CT into B Site. Clear CT B angle first, then the B Main entry side.

#### Adapting to T Reads
▸ If Ts are fast rushing B Main:
- B anchors play passively (deep B hold). IGL rotates B immediately.
- Do not fight in the B Main corridor — take the site crossfire instead.
▸ If Ts are slow-pushing Mid with Cave control:
- IGL uses utility to slow Cave. AWPer holds from A Short to punish any Mid push.
- B anchors stay patient — do not rotate until T commitment to one site is confirmed.
