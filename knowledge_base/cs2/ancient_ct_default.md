# CT-Side Default for de_ancient

#### Introduction
Ancient CT-side is built around Donut (Mid) control and positional discipline at both bombsites. The Donut structure gives CTs rotational access to both sites in seconds — losing Donut forces purely reactive site defense with long rotation paths. Core principle: the AWP owns Snipers' Nest (mid), an A Main molotov is thrown at round start every round, and Water / Tunnel paths are used for mid-round flanks that T-side rarely anticipates.

#### Opening Utility Usage (0-5s)
▸ [IGL/Mid]: Throw A Main molotov from CT A area landing at A Main entry arch — slows T A Main push and buys the A anchor time to set up crossfire position before Ts arrive ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [B Anchor]: Flash B Cave from B Site area — early flash into Cave to catch any T B-rushing through Cave entrance in the first seconds of the round ▶ Watch lineup.
▸ [AWPer/Mid Support]: Throw Donut molotov from CT Mid direction landing at Donut entry — delays Ts from gaining mid control through the Donut structure; gives AWPer time to take Snipers' Nest aggressively ▶ Watch lineup.

#### Default Positions
▸ [AWPer]: Play Snipers' Nest (Mid) — dominates mid; has long sightlines down the mid corridor and toward Cat; takes an aggressive early peek mid before T smokes arrive, then retreats inside.
▸ [Mid Support]: Hold Donut — supports AWPer; watches Cat and the A-side Donut exit; second mid anchor that can swing A quickly on a T push.
▸ [A Anchor]: Hold Temple or Main Box — watches A Main entry through the arch chokepoint; the A Main arch is a natural chokepoint that rewards tight angle-holding over wide peeking.
▸ [B Anchor 1]: Hold Cave — watches Cave entrance and back B Site corner; Cave is the tightest anchor position on the map — throw flash INTO Cave before entering from CT side to self-clear.
▸ [IGL / B Support]: Hold B Ramp or Long B — watches B Ramp entry and B Long approach; provides Long B / Pillar crossfire with the Cave anchor.

#### Rotation Rules
▸ If Snipers' Nest smoke thrown by Ts (T mid push imminent): AWPer falls back to Donut or CT area immediately; Donut player throws flash toward Cat and prepares the Cat engagement.
▸ If Cat pressure confirmed: Donut player throws flash and engages; AWPer re-angles from inside Donut.
▸ If A Main molotov burns out + heavy footsteps heard (A execute coming): Donut player rotates toward Temple to form a crossfire with A anchor; IGL calls B player to hold tight.
▸ If Cave push confirmed: B Ramp player collapses toward B Site; IGL from Long B provides second angle; both converge on Cave exit.
▸ If bomb planted B: AWP and Donut player descend through Alley or Tunnel path; A anchor stays to prevent T flank.
▸ If bomb planted A: B players rotate via Tunnel / Water path (~7 seconds); arrive in time for a coordinated retake.

#### Crossfire Setups
▸ Snipers' Nest + Donut (Mid): AWP holds long mid at Snipers' Nest; Donut player holds the short/rotational angle. A T pushing mid faces two very different engagement ranges simultaneously — long-range AWP and close-to-mid-range Donut — impossible to clear without dedicated utility.
▸ Temple + Donut exit (A Site entry): Temple player watches A Main left side from the arch; Donut player covers the right side from the Donut exit. T pushing A Main is caught in a crossfire where both angles are recessed or elevated differently.
▸ Cave + Long B / Pillar (B Site): Cave player holds Cave entrance; Long B / Ramp player holds the Ramp approach. The Cave entrance and Ramp approach are the two B entry paths — holding both simultaneously is the core B crossfire on Ancient.

#### Retake Coordination
▸ A Site Retake: Donut player enters A from Donut exit (fastest A approach from mid) — throws flash into site before entry. Temple player or CT rotator enters from A Main with a second flash 2 seconds later. Molotov the common A plant spot near Main box center to flush lurking Ts. Smoke CT Spawn exit to block T flank from behind. Donut player clears left (Temple side); A Main entry player clears right (Pillar/box area).
▸ B Site Retake: IGL (Long B) advances through Pillar angle. Cave player (if alive) delays from inside Cave with a flash pop. Molotov Cave exit area (common post-plant T position). Smoke Water passage or Back Hall to block T visibility toward the plant. Two-point entry: Ramp side and Long B side create a B Site pincer — Ts cannot hold both.

#### Adapting to T Reads
▸ If Ts are slow-pushing Mid with Cave / Elbow control:
- IGL throws utility (molotov or smoke) to slow Cave. AWPer holds from inside Snipers' Nest for the counter-peek.
- B anchors stay patient — do not rotate until T commitment to one site is confirmed by the Donut/AWPer read.
▸ If Ts are fast rushing B Main:
- B anchors play deep passive (do not fight in B Main corridor). IGL rotates B immediately from Long B.
- Donut player does NOT rotate B until Donut is secured from a second CT — leaving Donut empty opens the A execute window.
▸ If Ts are running an A 2-3 split (A Main + Donut):
- A anchor plays Temple tight — do not over-peek. Donut player holds the Donut exit to force Ts to commit fully before the A execute.
- AWPer re-angles from Snipers' Nest toward the A Main entry corridor after the Elbow smoke lands.

#### Pro-Level Nuances
▸ Donut is the map's brain: Losing Donut means CTs play pure reactive site defense. Pro CTs treat Donut like Mirage's Window — a non-negotiable control point defended every round.
▸ Heavy smoke reliance: Ancient has no long open sightlines — all engagements are close to medium range through chokepoints. Pro CTs use smokes to reduce multi-angle scenarios to single-duel scenarios.
▸ A Main arch is a natural chokepoint: Pro CTs use the arch geometry to hold tight angles with minimal exposure. Never wide-peek A Main — the arch rewards tight holds over aggressive plays.
▸ Water/Tunnel path is underused: Amateur CTs ignore the Water path between mid and B. Pro players use it for mid-round flanks and unexpected retake angles that catch Ts off-guard.
▸ Cave is the most dangerous anchor position: Cave is extremely tight — pro CTs throw a flash INTO Cave before entering from CT side to self-clear; never sit blind inside Cave against a T rushing the corner.
▸ A Main molotov mandatory every round: Slows T A push and buys CT A anchor 3–4 seconds of setup time. Skipping it is giving Ts free unchallenged A Main control.
