# Ancient — CT-Side Default

**Map overview (CT-perspective):** Ancient's CTs win through aggressive Donut control denying T mid access, and an AWP at A-Steps or Donut creating early picks. B-Main is slow enough that 2 CTs easily hold; A-Side needs 2–3 players because the execute distance from A-Main is very short.

---

## Starting Positions

| Role | Callout | Anchor Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| AWPer | A-Steps or Donut | Opens mid duel; punishes T-Mid aggression; repositions to A after pick |
| Entry | A-Site (cave side) | Aggressive A-Main peek; crossfire with AWPer from Steps |
| Support | A-Site (CT corner) | Anchors A with Entry; holds Temple exit angle |
| Lurker | B-Site | Anchors B-Main; calls T-B timing |
| IGL | Mid (Donut) OR CT Spawn | Reads mid control status; rotates to weakest site |

**Standard setup: 2 A (Entry + Support), 1 Donut/Mid (AWP), 1 B (Lurker), 1 IGL rotating**

---

## Early Map Control (1:40 → 1:10)

AWPer opens Donut at 1:42 — if T is slow to contest, AWPer establishes mid control, limiting T to A-Main or B-Main routes only. Entry peeks A-Main aggressively at 1:40. Lurker throws HE into B-Main at 1:50.

**Opening utility:**
- **Lurker** throws HE into **B-Main** at 1:50 to punish a fast B rush — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+ancient+he+b+main+ct)
- **Entry** flashes **A-Main** at 1:40 for an aggressive peek — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+ancient+flash+a+main+ct+peek)
- **IGL** can throw a molotov into **Cave** from **Donut** to deny T Cave control — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+ancient+molotov+donut+cave+ct)

## Rotation Rules

| Trigger | Response |
|---|---|
| AWPer picks T at Donut or T-Mid | AWPer repositions to Steps or A-Site; team holds standard |
| Lurker calls "smoke + flash B-Main" | IGL rotates from Donut to B; Lurker holds B close |
| Entry calls "A-Main smoke + Temple smoke" | AWPer immediately calls "rotating A — coming Steps"; IGL rotates from Mid to A-Side |
| Both A and B getting smoked simultaneously | Split — AWPer holds A, Lurker holds B, IGL stays CT-connector |
| IGL hears Donut footsteps (T mid control) | Entry calls A-Main aggression; Support watches Temple exit |

## Crossfire Setups

**Setup 1 — Steps + Cave (A-Site):**
- **AWPer** holds from **Steps** (long A angle)
- **Entry** holds from **Cave** (close left-side angle)
- T must win both simultaneously; Steps AWP angle is extremely punishing

**Setup 2 — CT Corner + Temple (A-Site):**
- **Support** holds from **CT corner**
- **IGL** holds from **Temple exit**
- Classic A crossfire; rotated entry from Temple forces T to re-clear CT side

**Setup 3 — B-Main chokepoint + Pillar (B-Site):**
- **Lurker** holds from **Pillar** (B-Site)
- **IGL** holds from **B-Main chokepoint angle**
- T entering B-Main must clear two angles at once from a narrow entry

---

## Retake — A Site

**Scenario:** T executes A via A-Main + Donut split with CT smoke, Temple smoke, Steps smoke.

1. **AWPer** waits for Steps smoke to dissipate, then re-enters Steps from **CT Spawn** — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+ancient+a+retake+steps+ct)
2. **Support** re-enters via **Temple** (smoke clears from Temple side)
3. **Entry** pops flash from **A-Main** (outside site) and re-enters
4. AWPer + Support retake together; Entry covers A-Main exit
5. **IGL** defuses from CT side behind pillar

---

## Retake — B Site

**Scenario:** T executes B-Main with CT and Pillar smokes.

1. **Lurker** holds **B-Site** passively — does not push into smoke, waits for it to clear
2. **IGL** rotates from **Donut → Mid → B-Side entry** — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+ancient+b+retake+mid+rotation)
3. **AWPer** rotates from A-Side through CT Spawn to B
4. Pop flash from CT Spawn side into B-Site; Lurker + IGL enter together
5. Entry stays A to deny any T lurking A-Main

## Economy

- **CT pistol:** Entry + Support buy armor for A defense. Lurker buys HE for B-Main. AWPer buys P250.
- **Anti-eco:** AWPer full buys; aggressive Donut AWP picks win anti-eco rounds alone.
- **Force Buy:** Coordinate — all AKs or all pistols. Never split the buy.
