# Nuke — T-Side Default

**Map overview (T-perspective):** Nuke is the most CT-sided map in the pool. T-side wins through aggressive Outside control (to prevent Heaven/Roof AWP domination), careful Ramp/Secret control, and then a coordinated Upper or Lower execute. Do not split 5 ways — always enter Upper or Lower together. The T AWP is most effective from Outside or Lobby.

---

## Starting Positions

| Role | Callout | Opening Job |
|---|---|---|
| AWPer | T-Spawn → Outside | Open Outside; contest Roof/Heaven AWP; enable Lobby control |
| Entry | T-Spawn → Outside (with AWPer) | Pushes into Hut/Lobby after AWPer wins Outside |
| Support | T-Spawn → Lobby | Smokes Heaven from Lobby; enables safe Lobby-to-Ramp push |
| Lurker | T-Spawn → Lobby → Secret | Takes Secret route; threatens Lower B independently |
| IGL | T-Spawn → Outside OR Lobby | Calls Upper vs Lower based on Outside control status |

**Default split: 3 Outside + 1 Secret (Lurker) + 1 Lobby (Support)**

---

## Default Phase (1:40 → 1:00)

AWPer controls Outside at 1:42 — a Heaven/Roof pick here is transformative, opening safe Ramp entry. If AWPer doesn't win Outside, Support smokes Heaven to safely push Lobby to Ramp. Lurker takes Secret independently and threatens Lower.

- **Support** smokes **Heaven** from **Lobby** at 1:40 to enable safe Ramp push — [▶ Watch lineup](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cs2+nuke+smoke+lobby+to+heaven)

## Economy Considerations

- **Pistol T (Round 1):** 4 push Outside together; Lurker takes Secret solo. Force a quick A execute — B is too risky on pistols alone.
- **Anti-Eco:** Rush Upper with 5; Outside control → Ramp drop. No need for utility.
- **Force Buy:** AK rush through Secret → Lower. Fastest route; skip smoke if not enough money.
